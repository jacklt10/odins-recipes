Poke Bowls

Things you will need:

  • Cooked Rice
  • Veggies of your choosing
  • Some kind of Protein

Now lets put it together

  1. Make a Bed of Rice
  2. Dice protein to 1cm cubes and place over one section of the bed of rice
  3. Slice veggies into 1cm cubes, or however you would prefer
  4. (optional) Garnish with soy sauce and sesame seeds
  5. Enjoy
