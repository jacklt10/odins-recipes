Vegan Bolognese

Go mental for lentils with this tasty meal

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choosing
  • Can of lentils
  • Carrots
  • Can of diced tomatos
  • mixed herbs

Steps

  1. Dice carrots and onions
  2. Sautee onions and carrots until brown
  3. Deglaze pan with redwine
  4. Add tin tomatos and strained lentils
  5. Add mixed herbs and leave to simmer
  6. Boil Pasta until Al dente and add 1 cup of pasta water to bolognese mix
  7. When bolognese is at desired consistency palte a bed of pasta and top with Bolognese
  8. (optional) Garnish with Chopped parsley or basil leaves
